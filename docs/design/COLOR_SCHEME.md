# The Missing Sock Photography - Color Scheme

Based on the official homepage design, this document defines the brand color palette and usage guidelines.

## üé® Brand Colors

### Primary Colors

#### #FF5E3F - Coral Orange (Primary CTA Buttons)
**Usage:**
- Primary call-to-action buttons
- "REGISTER FOR PICTURE DAY!" button in header
- "BOOK YOUR NEXT PICTURE DAY" button in hero section
- "I'm A School Director And Wish To Book A Picture Day" button
- Important action items that need high visibility

**Purpose:** Creates strong visual focal points and draws attention to key actions.

---

#### #ECE9E6 - Main Background Color
**Usage:**
- Primary page background color
- Header bar background
- Main content section backgrounds
- Provides clean, soft canvas for content

**Purpose:** Creates a warm, inviting, and professional base that allows other elements to stand out.

---

#### #F1BF61 - Accent Golden Yellow
**Usage:**
- Section backgrounds (hero sections, footer)
- Button backgrounds ("LEARN MORE ABOUT US", "READ WHAT PEOPLE ARE SAYING")
- Text color in white buttons ("CHECK OUT OUR PORTFOLIO", "I Want To Book A Family Session")
- Footer background
- Accent highlights and secondary CTAs

**Purpose:** Adds warmth and visual interest as a secondary accent color. Used for softer calls-to-action and decorative elements.

---

#### #FFFFFF - White
**Usage:**
- Text color on colored backgrounds (hero headings, testimonials)
- Button backgrounds (secondary buttons with colored text)
- High-contrast text for readability
- Clean, bright elements

**Purpose:** Ensures high readability and creates clean, bright aesthetic. Used extensively for text on colored backgrounds.

## üìê Color Usage Patterns

### Button Styles

1. **Primary CTA Button** (Coral Orange)
   - Background: `#FF5E3F`
   - Text: `#FFFFFF` (white)
   - Use: Main actions like "Register", "Book Now", "Start Pre-Order"

2. **Secondary CTA Button** (White with Accent Text)
   - Background: `#FFFFFF` (white)
   - Text: `#F1BF61` (accent golden yellow)
   - Border: Optional thin border in accent color
   - Use: Secondary actions like "View Portfolio", "Learn More"

3. **Accent Button** (Golden Yellow)
   - Background: `#F1BF61` (accent golden yellow)
   - Text: `#FFFFFF` (white)
   - Use: Secondary CTAs, footer buttons

### Section Backgrounds

1. **Hero Sections**
   - Can use `#F1BF61` (accent golden yellow) for warm, inviting hero backgrounds
   - Text overlay in `#FFFFFF` (white) for contrast

2. **Main Content Sections**
   - Background: `#ECE9E6` (main background color)
   - Provides soft, clean base for content

3. **Alternating Sections**
   - Use `#FFFFFF` (white) and `#ECE9E6` (main background) for visual variety

### Typography Colors

- **Headings on colored backgrounds:** `#FFFFFF` (white)
- **Headings on light backgrounds:** Dark gray/black (`#2C3E50` or similar)
- **Body text:** Dark gray (`#34495E` or similar)
- **Accent text:** `#F1BF61` (accent golden yellow) for highlights

## üéØ Design Principles

### Warmth & Approachability
The color palette emphasizes warmth and approachability, perfect for a family-focused photography business:
- Coral orange (#FF5E3F) provides energy and excitement
- Golden yellow (#F1BF61) adds warmth and friendliness
- Soft beige (#ECE9E6) creates comfort and trust

### Professionalism
Despite the warm palette, the design maintains professionalism:
- Clean white backgrounds and text
- Consistent color usage
- High contrast for readability

### Visual Hierarchy
Colors are used strategically to guide user attention:
1. Coral orange (#FF5E3F) - Primary actions
2. Golden yellow (#F1BF61) - Secondary actions and accents
3. White (#FFFFFF) - Content and secondary buttons
4. Soft beige (#ECE9E6) - Backgrounds and neutral areas
